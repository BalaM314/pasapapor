Pasapapor: A CAIE Past Paper Opener

For those who are tired of copying the url into a new tab and changing "qp" to "ms"

Type the paper information with the following format: (subject) (season) (paper type) (component code)

  • (subject): the 4 digit subject code, or name(wip)
  • (season): the season followed by the last two digits of the year, like this: "w21"
  • (paper type): "qp" or "ms" or sometimes "in". If omitted, both qp and ms will be opened.
  • (component code): the 2 digit component code

You can also:

  • Open a grade thresholds file or examiner report like "9231 w22 gt"
  • Open mf10 or mf19
  • Select AS/A or IGCSE through the text box
